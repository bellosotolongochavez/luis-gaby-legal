# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application "Automatizaciones Luis Gaby" ("the App"), developed and operated by Luis Dan Bello ("the Developer"), handles information.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information from end users. The App only uses:

- The Developer's own TikTok account credentials (OAuth access token), used solely to publish content to the Developer's own account.
- Video files and captions created by the Developer for publication.

## 2. How Information Is Used

Information is used exclusively to authenticate with the TikTok API and publish content to the Developer's own TikTok account (@luis.bello992). No data is shared with third parties, sold, or used for advertising.

## 3. Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored as encrypted secrets in GitHub Actions and are not accessible to any party other than the Developer. Video files are temporarily stored in a private Cloudflare R2 bucket controlled by the Developer.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the TikTok Content Posting API. Use of this integration is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

## 5. Data Retention

Published video files are retained in storage for operational purposes and are deleted periodically. No user data beyond the Developer's own content is retained.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
